  • Name : Director, NIMHANS Bangalore
  • Designation: Ex-Officio Member
Details

Dr Pratima Murthy is the Director and Senior Professor of Psychiatry at the National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences (NIMHANS), Bengaluru.

A distinguished leader in addiction psychiatry, she has served as a consultant to prominent international agencies and is currently a member of the Strategic Technical Advisory Group (STAG) on mental health, neurological, and substance use disorders. Dr. Murthy is widely recognized for her contributions to improving care for persons with mental illness through her work with the National Human Rights Commission on quality assurance in mental health care. She has been providing leadership for the national Tele MANAS programme and the Brain Health Initiative at NIMHANS.

Her remarkable contributions have earned her numerous awards and honours, including the Dr. Raja Ramanna State Award, the Lifetime Achievement Award from Rotary, and the WHO Regional Director’s Special Recognition Award on World No Tobacco Day 2021. She has also made significant academic contributions, with over 300 research publications to her name.

She has been a guest speaker at more than 700 seminars and conferences and has conducted over 300 workshops. Additionally, she has appeared in mass media (television and radio) to disseminate knowledge on mental health problems, prevention, early identification, and treatment/management.

Currently, she is engaged in research projects focusing on substance abuse, neurocognitive functions across the lifespan, and the mental health of young children. Dr. Hazarika is also the founder of an institute called “MYND: Heal to Evolve,” which focuses on clinical, academic, and research aspects of mental health and psychosocial domains.
